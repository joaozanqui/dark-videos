**Your Mission:** Logo Creation Framework
Your task is to synthesize the inputs and construct a prompt for a logo that is simple, relevant, and memorable.

Distill the Core Concept: Analyze the channel to identify the single most important idea or symbol that represents the channel's essence.

Incorporate the Audience's Vibe: Use the audience's voice to determine the emotional tone. Should the logo feel trustworthy and professional, or energetic and exciting? Mysterious and deep, or clean and modern?

**Construct the Logo Prompt:** Combine these elements into a single, concise paragraph. Focus on logo-specific design principles. Your prompt must include:

**Design Style:** Specify a clear logo style. Examples: "Minimalist vector logo," "modern emblem," "flat icon design," "abstract geometric mark," "sleek mascot logo."
-Central Icon/Symbol: Describe the main visual element. This should be a single, strong, and easily identifiable object, letter, or abstract shape. Avoid complex scenes.
-Color Palette: Define a limited and impactful color scheme that reflects the channel's mood and audience's preferences. Examples: "a palette of deep blues and silver for a professional tech feel," "vibrant orange and white for an energetic and clear message," "an organic palette of forest green and earthy brown."
-Composition & Feel: Describe the overall aesthetic. Examples: "Clean and professional with sharp lines," "organic and flowing with soft curves," "bold and powerful," "minimalist and elegant."
-Background: Specify a simple background, usually "on a solid white background" or "on a solid black background" to ensure the logo is isolated and versatile.

**Output Requirements**
Your final output must be only the generated prompt in English.
Do not include any explanations, greetings, or any text other than the prompt itself.
The prompt must be a single, well-written paragraph, optimized for creating a clean, high-quality logo in an AI image generator.
